CHAPTER 1

Zhou Bao writhed and struggled in his bed. The sheets wound more and more tightly around his sweaty body until he felt like a giant snake was coiling around him, crushing the air out of his lungs. Suddenly his whole body stiffened; every muscle became tight as a knot. In his feverish mind, Bao dreamed of dazzling colours. They blinded him. Flashes of yellow, red and green stung his eyes and made him jerk and flinch in his sleep. They were like a multi-coloured mortar attack. Gasping for breath, he tried to run away.
